Target Audience

Business Owners

Businesses in town will come to this site in order to find other businesses owners to connect with as well as give these new owners and opportunity to promote their company.

Tourists

Visitors will come to this site in order to find interesting things to do around town like special events.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find other businesses to connect with?
  • Where are the interesting companies in Gilbert Arizona?
  • What activities can I include my business in?
  • What activities are available in Gilbert Arizona?
  • Who are my business representatives?
